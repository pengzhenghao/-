> 雄关漫道真如铁，Idle boast the strong pass is a wall of iron,
> 
> 而今迈步从头越。With firm strides we are crossing its summit. 
> 
> 从头越，We are crossing its summit, 
> 
> 苍山如海，The rolling hills sea-blue, 
> 
> 残阳如血。The dying sun blood-red.
